# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Welcome to the Future of Robotics

This comprehensive guide will take you on a journey through the fascinating world of Physical AI and humanoid robotics. Whether you're a beginner looking to understand the fundamentals or an intermediate developer seeking to build sophisticated robotic systems, this book provides the knowledge and practical skills you need.

## What You'll Learn

This book is structured into four comprehensive modules:

1. **ROS 2 – The Robotic Nervous System**: Master the middleware that connects robotic components
2. **Gazebo + Unity – The Digital Twin**: Explore simulation environments and digital twin concepts
3. **NVIDIA Isaac – The AI-Robot Brain**: Discover perception pipelines and robot control
4. **Vision-Language-Action (VLA)**: Integrate LLMs with ROS 2 for advanced robot control

## Target Audience

This book is designed for:
- Beginner-to-intermediate developers
- Robotics enthusiasts
- AI/ML practitioners interested in embodied intelligence
- Students and educators in robotics

## Prerequisites

- Basic programming knowledge (Python/C++)
- Understanding of fundamental robotics concepts (helpful but not required)
- Access to a computer capable of running ROS 2 and simulation environments

## How to Use This Book

Each lesson follows a consistent structure:
- **Overview**: What you'll learn in this lesson
- **Concepts**: Theoretical foundations
- **Steps**: Practical implementation guide
- **Code**: Sample code and examples
- **Examples**: Real-world applications
- **Best Practices**: Industry standards and recommendations
